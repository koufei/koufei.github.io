I am currently an algorithm expert and team manager at the Imaging Algorithm Research Department of vivo Mobile Communication Co. Ltd.. I received my Ph.D. from Beihang University (BUAA) in March 2017 and my Bachelor degree from University of Science and Technology Beijing (USTB) in June 2010. I was a visiting Ph.D. student in Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore from Oct. 2012 to Apr. 2013 and from July 2014 to July 2016.

I have focused on HDR imaging in both academia and industry since 2011. I am currently focused on image enhancement techniques related to tone, color, and stylization.
